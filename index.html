Our Legacy

40+ Years of Authentic
Nihari Mastery

Located in the heart of C1 Area, Liaquatabad, Karachi, Jumma Gujjar Nihari has been serving the city's most beloved Desi Ghee Nihari for over four decades.

From slow-cooked beef nihari simmered overnight to aromatic biryani and creamy lassi, every dish carries the legacy of traditional Pakistani street food. Our secret? Pure desi ghee, hand-ground spices, and patience.
